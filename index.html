為什麼需要練習

Why Practice

多數求職者在正式面試前，
從未有機會練習

Most Candidates Walk Into Interviews
Having Never Rehearsed

面試是一種技能，而不是天賦。但很少人有機會在安全的環境下反覆練習，直到真正上場才發現自己的問題。

Interviewing is a skill, not a talent — yet few candidates get to rehearse it in a low-stakes setting. Most only discover their weak points once it's too late to fix them.

🎯

練習機會難找

Practice Partners Are Hard to Find

找親友模擬面試，對方通常不了解 HR 的提問邏輯，也給不出專業回饋。有價值的練習機會很難取得。

Friends and family rarely understand how HR actually structures questions, and can't offer professional feedback. Meaningful practice is simply hard to come by.

🔍

不知道自己哪裡弱

Blind Spots Go Unnoticed

答完覺得還可以，但 HR 沒有回音。不清楚是回答內容不夠具體、邏輯不清，還是哪個環節出了問題。

An answer can feel fine in the moment, then go nowhere. Without a response from HR, there's no way to tell whether it was the substance, the logic, or something else entirely.

📋

面試後沒有具體回饋

Real Interviews Rarely Come With Feedback

正式面試結束後，鮮少能拿到具體的改善建議。每一次面試的成長，只能靠自己猜測與摸索。

Once a formal interview ends, specific feedback almost never follows. Any improvement has to come from guesswork.

A Real Conversation,
Not a Form to Fill Out

MockMate 以語音進行，就像真正的面試一樣。AI 面試官根據你上傳的履歷即時生成問題，並在你的回答不夠具體時，自然地追問下去。

MockMate runs entirely by voice, the way an actual interview would. The AI interviewer builds questions from your resume in real time, and follows up naturally whenever an answer needs more detail.

  • AI 面試官依你的履歷客製化提問，不是固定題庫
  • Questions are generated from your resume, not pulled from a fixed bank
  • 涵蓋自我介紹、工作經歷、離職原因、職涯規劃、薪資期望等必問題目
  • Covers the questions every interview asks: your background, work history, reason for leaving, career plans, salary expectations
  • 靜音偵測自動送出，不需手動操作，專注在回答即可
  • Silence detection submits your answer automatically — no buttons to press, just talk
  • 最多 20 輪對話，完整模擬真實面試節奏
  • Up to 20 exchanges, paced to match a real interview

Use of Examples

回答「帶領團隊」時未說明人數、時程與具體成果，建議用 STAR 原則重新組織。

The team-leadership answer left out team size, timeline, and concrete results — restructuring it with the STAR method would help.

Career Narrative

The connection between the reason for leaving and future plans needs to be clearer — right now the two feel loosely tied together.

面試官可能的觀察
What an Interviewer Might Notice

回答壓力情境時出現多次「應該」、「大概」等模糊用詞，可能讓面試官感知到不確定感。

Hedging language — "probably," "I think" — came up repeatedly under pressure questions, which can read as uncertainty.

改善建議（節錄）
To Improve (Selected)
  • 準備 2–3 個 STAR 格式的具體事例，可用於多題
  • Prepare two to three STAR-format examples that can flex across multiple questions
  • 離職原因改用「正向拉力」陳述，而非「負向推力」
  • Frame the reason for leaving around what you're moving toward, not what you're escaping
  • 職涯規劃加入對應徵職位的連結，說明為何是此公司
  • Tie career plans explicitly to the role — explain why this company, specifically
